💡 WHAT YOU'LL SEE

✅ 45+ pre-loaded verified sources including:
   • State temple authorities (HR&CE, TTD, Devaswom Boards, etc.)
   • Official temple websites (Meenakshi, Jagannath, Somnath, etc.)
   • Heritage authorities (ASI, Ministry of Tourism, etc.)

✅ Working features:
   • Search sources by name

   • Filter by status (verified, pending, unverified)

   • Edit/Delete buttons
