PROBLEM:
--------
Mobile app was not loading due to missing screen imports in App.tsx

SYMPTOMS:
---------
• App crashed on startup
• "Cannot find module" errors
• Blank screen in browser
• Navigation not working

ROOT CAUSE:
-----------
App.tsx had outdated imports referencing screens that:
1. No longer existed (TempleListScreen, ArtifactDetailScreen, ProfileScreen)
2. Were renamed (TempleDetailScreen → TempleDetailsScreen)
3. Were missing from navigation (LanguageSelection, IndiaMap, etc.)

-----------------
✅ Fixed all screen imports in App.tsx
✅ Corrected screen name mismatches
✅ Added missing screens to navigation stack
