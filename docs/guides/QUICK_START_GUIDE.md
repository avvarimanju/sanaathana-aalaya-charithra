## 🎯 What This App Does (In Simple Terms)

**Think of it as:** Your personal AI tour guide for Hindu temples

**What it does:**
- Shows you information about 11 Hindu temples
- Provides audio guides, videos, and detailed history
- Works with QR codes placed at temple artifacts
- Available in 10+ Indian languages

**What it does NOT do:**
- ❌ Does NOT recognize any random photo/sculpture
- ❌ Does NOT work without QR codes
- ❌ Only works for 23 specific artifacts in 11 temples

---

## 🚀 Two Ways to Use

### Option 1: From Home (Before Visiting)
1. Download app
2. Browse 11 temples
3. Read basic info (FREE)
4. Unlock temple for ₹99 to get full content
5. Plan your visit

### Option 2: At Temple (During Visit)
1. Find QR code near artifact
2. Scan with app
3. Get preview (FREE)
4. Unlock for ₹99 to access full content
5. Listen to audio guide
