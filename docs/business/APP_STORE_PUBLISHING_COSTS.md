# App Store Publishing Costs
## Google Play Store & Apple App Store Fees

---

## 🎯 Your Question

**"Do we have to pay money for Android Store or Play Store while publishing the app?"**

Yes, there are fees involved. Let me break down all costs.

---

## 📱 Google Play Store (Android)

### **One-Time Registration Fee**

**Cost:** $25 USD (₹2,075 approximately)

**Details:**
- Pay once, publish unlimited apps forever
- Lifetime developer account
- No annual renewal fees
- No hidden costs

**Payment Methods:**
- Credit card
- Debit card
- Google Pay

**Process:**
1. Create Google Play Console account
2. Pay $25 one-time fee
3. Wait 24-48 hours for verification
4. Start publishing apps

### **Ongoing Costs**

**App Publishing:** FREE ✅
- Upload unlimited apps
- Update apps anytime
- No per-app fees
- No monthly fees

**In-App Purchases (If you use Google Play Billing):**
- 15% commission (first $1M revenue per year)
- 30% commission (after $1M revenue)

**Example:**
```
If you use Google Play In-App Billing:
- User pays ₹99
- Google takes ₹15-30 (15-30%)
- You receive ₹69-84

If you use external payment (Razorpay):
- User pays ₹99
- Razorpay takes ₹2 (2%)
- You receive ₹97
- Google takes ₹0 ✅
```

### **Total Cost for Google Play Store**

**Initial:** $25 (₹2,075) one-time  
**Annual:** ₹0  
**Per App:** ₹0  
**Updates:** ₹0  

---

## 🍎 Apple App Store (iOS)

### **Annual Enrollment Fee**

**Cost:** $99 USD per year (₹8,217 approximately)

**Details:**
- Must renew every year
- Required to keep apps published
- If you don't renew, apps are removed
- Covers unlimited apps

**Payment Methods:**
- Credit card
- Debit card
- Apple Pay

**Process:**
1. Enroll in Apple Developer Program
2. Pay $99 annual fee
3. Wait for approval (1-2 days)
4. Start publishing apps

### **Ongoing Costs**

**App Publishing:** Included in $99/year
- Upload unlimited apps
- Update apps anytime
- No per-app fees

**In-App Purchases (If you use Apple In-App Purchase):**
- 15% commission (first $1M revenue per year)
- 30% commission (after $1M revenue)

**Example:**
```
If you use Apple In-App Purchase:
- User pays ₹99
- Apple takes ₹15-30 (15-30%)
- You receive ₹69-84

If you use external payment (Razorpay):
- User pays ₹99
- Razorpay takes ₹2 (2%)
- You receive ₹97
- Apple takes ₹0 ✅
```

### **Total Cost for Apple App Store**

**Initial:** $99 (₹8,217) first year  
**Annual:** $99 (₹8,217) every year  
**Per App:** ₹0  
**Updates:** ₹0

## 🎯 Break-Even Analysis

### **With Android Only**

**Fixed Costs Year 1:** ₹50,575

**Revenue Needed (at ₹99 per temple):**
```
Break-even: ₹50,575 ÷ ₹97 (after Razorpay fee) = 522 temple purchases

Or: 522 ÷ 12 months = 44 purchases per month
Or: 44 ÷ 2 temples per user = 22 users per month
```

**Conclusion:** Need 22 paying users per month to break even

### **With Android + iOS**

**Fixed Costs Year 1:** ₹58,292

**Revenue Needed:**
```
Break-even: ₹58,292 ÷ ₹97 = 601 temple purchases

Or: 601 ÷ 12 months = 51 purchases per month
Or: 51 ÷ 2 temples per user = 26 users per month
```

**Conclusion:** Need 26 paying users per month to break even
