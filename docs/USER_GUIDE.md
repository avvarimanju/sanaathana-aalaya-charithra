## 📱 What This App Does

Sanaathana Aalaya Charithra is a mobile app that provides rich, AI-generated content about Hindu temples and their artifacts. Think of it as your personal tour guide for Hindu heritage sites.

- ✅ Plan your visit before going

## 🎯 Two Ways to Use the App

### Way 1: Explore from Home (Before Visiting)

**Perfect for:**
- Planning your temple visit
- Learning about temples beforehand
- Virtual exploration
- Deciding which temples to visit

**How it works:**
1. Download app from Play Store
2. Open app → Select language
3. Tap "Explore Temples"
4. Browse list of 11 temples
5. Tap any temple to see details
6. Read about history, architecture, significance
7. See list of artifacts in that temple
8. Decide if you want to unlock premium content (₹99)

**What you get for FREE:**
- Temple names and locations
- Basic temple information
- List of artifacts
- Preview of content

**What requires payment (₹99 per temple):**
- Full audio guides
- Detailed historical narratives
- Videos and infographics
- Q&A chat feature
- Offline access

---

### Way 2: Use at Temple (During Visit)

**Perfect for:**
- Getting information while at the temple
- Learning about specific artifacts
- Enhanced temple experience
- Guided tour experience

**How it works:**
1. Visit one of the 11 supported temples
2. Find artifacts with QR codes
3. Open app → Tap "Scan QR Code"
4. Point camera at QR code
5. App identifies the artifact
6. See preview of content
7. Unlock temple for ₹99 to access full content
8. Listen to audio guide
9. Watch videos
10. Ask questions via chat

**What you need:**
- Internet connection (for first-time content download)

## 🔍 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General Questions

**Q: Is this app free?**
A: App download is free. Browsing temples is free. Full content requires ₹99 per temple.

**Q: Can I use this app for any temple?**
A: No, only for 11 pre-registered temples. More temples will be added in future updates.

**Q: Can I take a photo of any sculpture and get information?**
A: No, this app uses QR codes, not photo recognition. You must scan the QR code placed near the artifact.

**Q: Can I use this app from home?**
A: Yes! You can browse temples, read basic info, and unlock content from home. You don't need to be at the temple.

**Q: Do I need internet?**
A: Yes, for first-time content download and QR scanning. After download, content works offline.

### Payment Questions

**Q: How much does it cost?**
A: ₹99 per temple for 30-day access. One-time payment per temple.

**Q: Do I need to pay for each artifact?**
A: No, ₹99 unlocks ALL artifacts in that temple.

**Q: Can I pay with UPI?**
A: Yes, UPI, cards, net banking, and wallets are supported.

**Q: Is payment secure?**
A: Yes, powered by Razorpay with bank-level security.

**Q: What if I visit multiple temples?**
A: You pay ₹99 per temple. If you visit 3 temples, total cost is ₹297.

### Content Questions

**Q: What languages are supported?**
A: 10+ Indian languages including English, Hindi, Telugu, Bengali, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Marathi, Punjabi, Tamil, and more.

**Q: How long is the audio guide?**
A: 3-7 minutes per artifact, depending on complexity.

**Q: Can I ask unlimited questions?**
A: Yes, Q&A chat has no limits once you unlock the temple.

**Q: Is the content accurate?**
A: Content is AI-generated based on verified historical sources. We strive for accuracy but recommend cross-checking for academic purposes.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which phones are supported?**
A: Android 8.0 or higher. iOS version coming soon.

**Q: How much storage space needed?**
A: ~50-100 MB per temple. Total ~1 GB for all 11 temples.

**Q: Can I use on tablet?**
A: Yes, works on Android tablets.

**Q: What if QR code doesn't scan?**
A: Make sure camera permission is enabled. Try better lighting. Clean the QR code if dirty.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### App Won't Open
- Restart your phone
- Check if Android 8.0 or higher
- Reinstall app from Play Store
- Clear app cache

### QR Code Won't Scan
- Enable camera permission in settings
- Ensure good lighting
- Hold phone steady
- Clean QR code if dirty
- Try from different angle

### Payment Failed
- Check internet connection
- Verify payment details
- Try different payment method
- Contact support if issue persists

### Content Not Loading
- Check internet connection
- Restart app
- Clear app cache
- Redownload content

### Audio Not Playing
- Check volume settings
- Ensure headphones connected properly
- Try different audio file
- Restart app
