HOW TO MESSAGE YOURSELF ON WHATSAPP
====================================

METHOD 1: Click to Chat (Easiest)
----------------------------------
1. Open this link on your phone browser:
   https://wa.me/YOUR_PHONE_NUMBER
   
   Example: https://wa.me/919876543210
   (Use country code, no + or spaces)

2. It opens WhatsApp with a chat to yourself
3. Send screenshot to yourself
4. Open WhatsApp on desktop
5. Find that chat - screenshot is there!

METHOD 2: Save Your Own Number as Contact
------------------------------------------
1. Save your own phone number as a contact
   Name it: "Me" or "My Notes"
2. Open WhatsApp
3. Search for that contact
4. Send screenshot to yourself
5. Open WhatsApp desktop - it's there!

METHOD 3: WhatsApp Web Direct
------------------------------
1. Open web.whatsapp.com on desktop
2. Scan QR code with phone
3. On phone: Take screenshot
4. On phone: Open WhatsApp, find any chat
5. Click attach (📎) → Gallery → Select screenshot
6. Before sending, change recipient to yourself
7. Or just send to any chat and forward to yourself

EVEN EASIER: Use Telegram!
---------------------------
Telegram has built-in "Saved Messages":
1. Install Telegram on phone and desktop
2. Open "Saved Messages" (it's always there)
3. Send screenshot to Saved Messages
4. Instantly appears on desktop
5. No tricks needed!
