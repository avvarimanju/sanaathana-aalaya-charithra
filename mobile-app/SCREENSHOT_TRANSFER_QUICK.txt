PHONE TO DESKTOP SCREENSHOT TRANSFER
====================================

EASIEST METHOD (NO SETUP):
1. Connect phone to laptop with USB cable
2. Open File Explorer (Windows Explorer)
3. Click on your phone name
4. Go to: Internal Storage → DCIM → Screenshots
5. Copy screenshots to Desktop
6. Done!

ALTERNATIVE (NO CABLE):
1. Send screenshot to yourself on WhatsApp
2. Open WhatsApp on desktop
3. Download screenshot
4. Done!

WHY USE EMULATOR?
✅ Screenshots already on desktop (instant)
✅ Easy to share
✅ Fast for development

WHY USE REAL PHONE?
✅ Real performance testing
✅ Actual touch experience
✅ Real network/battery behavior

BEST PRACTICE:
- Daily development: Use emulator
- Final testing: Use real phone
- Both are important!
