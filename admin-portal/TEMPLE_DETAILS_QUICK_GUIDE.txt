2. Click "View Details" on any temple
3. See complete information!

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  WHAT YOU'LL SEE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TEMPLE HEADER:
  • Large temple image
  • Temple name & status
  • Location (city, district, state)
  • Deity name
  • Quick stats (artifacts, scans)

THREE TABS:

  📋 OVERVIEW TAB
     • Full description
     • Location details (address, pincode)
     • Contact info (phone, email, hours)
     • Special features
     • Timeline (created, updated)

  📿 ARTIFACTS TAB
     • List of all artifacts
     • QR codes
     • Scan counts
     • Status badges

  📊 ANALYTICS TAB
     • Total QR scans
     • Active artifacts count
     • Average scans per artifact
