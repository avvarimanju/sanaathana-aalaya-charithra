WIKIDATA (RECOMMENDED PRIMARY SOURCE)

✅ URL: https://query.wikidata.org
✅ Type: Structured knowledge database
✅ Coverage: 800+ Hindu temples in India
✅ Quality: 7/10 (community-verified, needs validation)
✅ Automation: EXCELLENT (SPARQL API)
✅ Cost: FREE
✅ Trusted: Wikimedia Foundation (community-curated)

Why Wikidata is BETTER than Wikipedia:
  • Structured data (easy to query)
  • Machine-readable (JSON, CSV)
  • Automated extraction (SPARQL)
  • Includes coordinates, images, multilingual names
  • Links to Wikipedia articles for details
  • Community-verified and maintained

Why NOT Wikipedia directly:
  • Unstructured text (hard to parse)
  • Manual extraction required
  • Inconsistent formatting
  • Time-consuming
